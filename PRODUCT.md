# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People exploring MBTI types, usually comparing personality descriptions and trait balances across the 16 types on desktop or mobile.

## Product Purpose

Help users browse, search, and compare MBTI types quickly, with a clear path from overview to detail without visual noise.

## Brand Personality

Calm, curious, precise. The app should feel clear and trustworthy, not decorative or overly playful.

## Anti-references

Overdone gradients, glassmorphism, neon sci-fi styling, cluttered card stacks, and any UI that makes simple browsing feel ceremonial.

## Design Principles

- Make comparisons easy at a glance.
- Keep the interface familiar and low-friction.
- Use one accent language consistently for actions and state.
- Support both light and dark mode with equal care.
- Favor clarity and scanability over decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-friendly controls, reduced-motion support, and readable text on both themes.
